Cycling Tour Package

  • Durations: 07 Nights / 08 Days

Day 01: Bangalore - Mysore (Drive approx. 145 Km)
Arrive in Bangalore and transfer to Mysore. Mysore, a city rich in culture and heritage is an excellent option to explore and visit. In the evening, visit the Mysore Palace. Overnight at the hotel in Mysore.

Day 02: Mysore - Bylakuppe - Coorg
(Drive approx. 90 Km, Cycle approx. 40 Km)

After breakfast, drive to Bylakuppe followed by visit to the Golden Temple. The cycling starts here. This will be a short day of cycling to familiarize the guest with the cycles.
Overnight at the hotel in Coorg.

Day 03: Coorg - Hassan (Cycle approx. 100 Km)
Cycling to Hassan through coffee plantations on quiet roads leaving behind the major traffic. Overnight at the hotel in Hassan.

Day 04: Hassan - Kaimara (Cycle approx. 70 Km)
Cycle to the coffee country of Karnataka. En-route visit the Halebeedu Temple, a World heritage site and also the temple at Belavadi. The overnight stay will be at a home stay nestled among coffee plantations.
Overnight at the hotel in Kaimara

Day 05: Kaimara - Balur (Cycle approx. 60 Km)
Continue cycling through the coffee country and it will be an easy day as we descend towards Mudigere and head to another beautiful coffee plantation bungalow.

Overnight at the hotel in Balur

Day 06: Balur - Koppa (Cycle approx. 60 Km)
Today cycle through a lot of plantations along the way and stay overnight surrounded by coffee plantations.
Overnight at the hotel in Koppa

Day 07: Koppa - Manipal (Cycle approx. 85 Km)
Pass through Agumbe today, one of the highest rainfall receiving regions in the Southern India. The guests will be descending to the plains today and stay overnight at Manipal. Cycling ends here. The Manipal beach is only a short distance drive from the hotel.
Overnight at the hotel in Manipal

Day 08: Manipal - Mangalore
Transfer to Mangalore Airport for flight to onward destination.
